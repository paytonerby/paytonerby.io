All About It

Modern Focal Point Photography is a company that explores the creativity behind the image. This lets student athletes see their actions in a result to an edit. We find the story and emotion behind each play, each score, and each athlete. Not only does this showcase student athletes in edited results.

I am Payton Erby. I am an artist who likes to search for the stories behind a picture. I got into photography the moment I took my first up close-up photo of wet grass. I was 13 when I started to explore the art of photography, and at that moment, I knew that this was my dream. I got my first camera when I was 15 and I use that camera to capture the story behind each sport picture and tell a beautiful story with the background I snap.
