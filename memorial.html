The Anti-Shark Club is honoring surfer Tamayo Perry. Perry was a lifeguard for the City and Country of Honolulu Ocean Safety and a surfer from O'ahu, Hawaii. Perry passed away at the age of 49 after being attacked by a shark while surfing, where he lost an arm and leg.

He was near Goat Island when he was attacked. In Honolulu, Hawaii, Honolulu Ocean Safety members responded to a call about a surfer being injured by a shark. First responders pronounced his death when they dragged him back to shore.

Perry was well known and loved by many in the state of Hawaii and in the surfer world. He holds a huge impact in the surfer community. He was celebrated for his skills in surfing.
